What is ERR_404 6.0?

ERR_404 is a nationally recognized two-day Hackathon organized by the Computer Department of Anjuman-I-Islam M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering.

Calling all cyber enthusiasts and tech-savvy learners! This is your chance to step forward, seize the opportunity, showcase your exceptional skills, and transform your innovative ideas into reality in a competitive atmosphere.

The hackathon places a strong emphasis on developing interpersonal skills such as teamwork, leadership, meticulousness, adaptability, and the ability to brainstorm ideas while successfully creating and executing a functional prototype.

Whether you're a first-time hackathon participant or a seasoned veteran with numerous hackathons under your belt, we welcome students of all skill levels and from all colleges to join us for a weekend filled with creativity, collaboration, and coding.

Machine Learning

Machine learning falls under the category of artificial intelligence (AI) and empowers computers to acquire knowledge without the need for explicit programming. It centers around creating computer algorithms that can adapt and evolve based on new data they encounter.

Web Development

Web development encompasses the tasks associated with creating websites for the Internet or private networks. This can span from designing a basic, static web page with plain text to crafting intricate web applications, online businesses, and social networking platforms.

Cloud Computing

Cloud computing refers to the convenient access to computer system resources, including data storage and processing capabilities, without requiring direct user intervention. Typically, it pertains to the utilization of data centers that serve numerous users via the Internet.

Big Data

Big data analytics involves the exploration of extensive and diverse datasets, commonly referred to as big data. Its aim is to discover concealed patterns, unanticipated connections, market trends, customer preferences, and valuable insights that enable organizations to make better-informed business choices.

Networking And Security

Network security is a specialized area within computer networking that focuses on safeguarding a computer network's infrastructure. Delve into the realm of network security, where you can develop inventive products or uncover undiscovered vulnerabilities. Does the term ETERNALBLUE ring a bell?

App Development

Mobile app development refers to the creation and construction of mobile applications designed for use on devices like smartphones, personal digital assistants (PDAs), or enterprise digital assistants.

Rules and regulations!

Teams may consist of four to five members. In order to maintain fairness among all participants, all code, design elements, and assets must be developed exclusively during the Hackathon's duration. Nonetheless, you are welcome to engage in pre-event planning, create wireframes, and brainstorm ideas. Additionally, you are permitted to utilize third-party services, APIs, open-source projects, libraries, and frameworks.
Alcohol and smoking are strictly prohibited at the event. Please maintain a professional demeanor.
Please be aware that at this event, any form of harassment, as well as jokes that are racist, sexist, or exclusionary, is considered unacceptable*. Those who attend and do not adhere to these guidelines may be requested to leave the Hackathon, solely at the discretion of the event organizers. We appreciate your cooperation in ensuring that this remains an inclusive and friendly gathering for everyone.

FAQ's


  • Who can participate?
    Anyone with a knack of programming and solving problems can participate.
  • How much does it cost?
    Thanks to our college, attendance is completely free. We will also provide you with WiFi, breakfast, lunch, snacks, dinner and a workspace.
  • What should I bring?
    Bring your laptop, charger and other gadgets suitable to your project and a will to code. Please bring a valid ID for admission.
  • Where is it?
    M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering, Byculla is happy to provide space in their campus to organize the Hackathon. You can find the directions in the Venue section.
  • Will Jain Food be provided?
    Yes, food arrangements will be made for Jain Participants.
  • Is it compulsory that all members belong to same college?
    No it is not, you can form a team of students from different colleges. While registering you can either put the Team Leader's College or mutually decide.
